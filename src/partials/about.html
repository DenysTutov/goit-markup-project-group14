Interesting fact

The first ice cream recipe was opened and declassified for widespread publication in the XI century BC in the book "Shi Jing" in China more than 4000 years ago. Its essence was that fresh snow or ice mixed with slices of lemons, oranges and pomegranate seeds.

What is ice cream made of?

Ice cream production requires several available and common components. The main ingredient is milk, which can be in liquid or powder form. Depending on the variety and price of the delicacy, one or both are used together. To give ice cream additional qualities and properties, sweeteners, dyes, stabilizers and fillings are added to its composition. In each variety they are different and combined.
